Get a Library Card

Get access to all the services services that are available from the King County Library System.

Fill out the application below to apply for your All-Access Library Card.

You'll need to bring proof of address and photo ID that shows your date of birth bring proof of address and photo ID that shows your date of birth to a library to activate your card. (Parents or guardians can help with this for applicants younger than age 18.)

Reasonable accommodation for individuals with disabilities is available upon request. Accommodations include modifications to policies, programs, appropriate equipment, and services. Contact your community library community library or visit https://kcls.org/accessibility/ to request accommodation.
